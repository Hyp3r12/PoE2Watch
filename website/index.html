PoE2Watch

♦

Never wonder if your trade sold again.

A self-hosted bot for sale alerts, item cards, goals, diagnostics, and trade stats.

Run it on your own machine, get Discord pings when sales are detected, and keep a searchable local history of your trades while official GGG OAuth support remains pending.

  • v0.6 alpha
  • Docker supported
  • MIT licensed
  • Local first

Self-hosted. Open source. Read-only. No gameplay automation.

Features

♦
N

Mobile Sale Pings

Get clean Discord notification text for meaningful sales while every sale still posts its full item card.

I

Item Cards

See rarity-colored item cards with icons, requirements, corrupted states, and useful modifier detail.

G

Goals & Analytics

Track goals, top sales, recent history, league totals, insights, averages, and estimated Divine value.

D

Docker & Diagnostics

Use the hardened Docker path, startup checklist, and sanitized health export when setup needs help.

Setup Path

♦

Command guide

Built for the moment you step away

Sale pings that make sense on your phone.

PoE2Watch keeps the mobile notification simple: the item name, sale price, and Discord channel context. The detailed item card still lives in Discord when you open the message.

  • Clean lock-screen summary for quick checks.
  • Full item details stay in Discord for when you have time.
  • Works with mirrored webhook channels for proof-of-concept sharing.
