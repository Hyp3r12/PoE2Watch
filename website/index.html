Discord companion for Path of Exile 2

PoE2Watch

◆

Never wonder if your trade sold again.

A Discord companion for Path of Exile 2 traders.

Get sale notifications, review your trading history, inspect hover-style item cards, and turn raw trade logs into clean Discord analytics.

Download Community Edition View on GitHub

Self-hosted. Open source. Built for the community.

Features

◆
N

Instant Notifications

Get notified in Discord when your items sell so valuable trades do not disappear into silence.

A

Powerful Analytics

Track totals, averages, largest sales, top trades, insights, and league performance over time.

S

Fully Customizable

Choose display currencies, refresh third-party estimates, and tune the companion around your trade flow.

H

Self Hosted

Run PoE2Watch on your own machine. Your trade history stays local and under your control.
